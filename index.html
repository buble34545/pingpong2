Ping Pong Game











Developed by: Filip Wabno

Instructions

  1. First keep your laptop screen straight
  2. Move youself approximately 3-4 feet away from the laptop
  3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and descending manner, a red dot should appear on your right wrist
  4. Now press the play button, and again move youself 3-4 feet away from the laptop
  5. The redd padel is your padel, it will move as per the movement of your right wrist
